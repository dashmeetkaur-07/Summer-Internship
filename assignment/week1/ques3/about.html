About Geeta University

Geeta University is a private university located in Panipat, Haryana (India). It was established around 2022 and offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across multiple fields.

Facilities:

  • Smart classrooms & labs
  • Library with digital resources
  • Hostel (separate for boys & girls)
  • Sports & gym
  • Wi-Fi campus

Departments:

  1. School of Computer Science & Engineering
    • B.Tech. Hons. CSE (Computer Science & Engineering)
    • B.Tech. Hons. CSE (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)
    • B.Tech. Hons. CSE (Cyber Security)
    • B.Tech. Hons. CSE (Full Stack Web Development)
    • M.Tech. CSE (Computer Science & Engineering)
    • Ph.D. CSE (Computer Science & Engineering)
  2. School of Commerce & Business Management
    • BBA Hons. (Bachelor of Business Administration)
    • BBA Hons. (International Accounting with ACCA UK)
    • MBA (Marketing)
    • MBA (Human Resource Management)
    • MBA (Supply Chain Management)
    • Ph.D. (Business & Management)
  3. School of Agricultural Studies
    • B.Sc. Hons. (Agriculture)
    • M.Sc. Agriculture (Agronomy)
    • Ph.D. (Agriculture)
  4. School of Hospitality & Hotel Management
    • Diploma in Hotel Management (Residential Program)
    • B.Sc. (Hons.) Hotel Management
    • M.Sc. Hotel Management
    • Ph.D. Hotel Management
  5. School of Humanities & Social Sciences
