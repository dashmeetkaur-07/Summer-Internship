Instructions

  1. Read all the instructions carefully before filling the registration form.
  2. Ensure that all personal and academic details entered are accurate.
  3. Select subjects carefully; changes may not be allowed after submission.
  4. Do not leave any mandatory field empty.
  5. Upload a recent passport-size photograph in the required format.

Eligibility

  • The candidate must be a registered student of the institution.
  • The student must have minimum 75% attendance in all subjects.
  • All tuition and examination fees must be cleared before registration.
  • The student must possess a valid roll number and student ID.
  • There should be no pending disciplinary actions against the student.
